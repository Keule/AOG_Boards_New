=== HTTP Endpunkte ===
GET  /logs              — Kompletter Ringbuffer-Inhalt (text/plain)
GET  /logs?tail=N       — Letzte N Zeilen (Standard: 50, Max: 500)
GET  /logs/status       — JSON: lines_written, lines_dropped, bytes_written, bytes_overwritten, buffer_size, used_bytes
POST /logs/clear        — Ringbuffer leeren (Statistiken werden zurückgesetzt)
GET  /logs/view         — Auto-refresh HTML Log-Viewer (1s Intervall, tail=200)
